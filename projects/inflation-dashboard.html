Pricing & Inflation Variance Dashboard

A finance analytics dashboard designed to monitor raw material inflation, pricing variance, COGS movement, and entity-level cost drivers.

Business Problem

Finance stakeholders needed a centralized way to track inflation and deflation impact across business groups, entities, materials, and fiscal periods.

Solution

Built a Power BI dashboard with overview, raw material cost inflation, and price-vs-COGS analysis pages. The dashboard supported drill-down investigation from executive summary to material and entity-level detail.

Key Features

  • YTD inflation by business group and entity
  • Monthly and cumulative inflation trend analysis
  • Raw material spend and inflation by category
  • Price vs. COGS comparison for customer-SKU analysis
  • Source-data validation against Databricks/SQL views

Business Impact

Helped finance and data engineering teams separate dashboard logic issues from upstream data availability issues, improving validation and root-cause analysis.

Screenshots and business values are not publicly displayed because this dashboard uses confidential company data. A sanitized walkthrough can be discussed upon request.
