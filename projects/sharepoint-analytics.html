SharePoint Analytics Dashboard

A Microsoft 365 analytics dashboard designed to track SharePoint usage, employee engagement, portal adoption, and content performance.

Business Problem

Business stakeholders needed visibility into SharePoint adoption, internal portal activity, user group engagement, and frequently accessed content.

Solution

Developed a Power BI reporting solution that analyzed SharePoint activity and converted portal usage data into actionable engagement insights.

Key Features

  • Monthly visitors by user group
  • Top accessed articles and pages
  • Internal portal usage reporting
  • User group segmentation
  • SharePoint adoption monitoring
  • Content engagement analysis

Business Impact

Helped stakeholders understand how employees interacted with internal SharePoint content and identify opportunities to improve communication, visibility, and adoption.

This project contains internal Microsoft 365 and SharePoint usage data. Screenshots and sensitive portal metrics are not publicly displayed.
